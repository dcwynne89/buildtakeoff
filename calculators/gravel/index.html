How to Calculate Cubic Yards of Gravel

Gravel is sold by the ton or cubic yard depending on the supplier. This calculator gives you both so you can order accurately regardless of how your supplier prices material.

The Formula

Volume (cu ft) = Length × Width × (Depth ÷ 12). Convert to cubic yards by dividing by 27. Then multiply cubic yards by the material's density to get tons.

Recommended Depths by Application

  • Driveway base layer — 4"–6" of compacted crushed stone
  • Driveway top layer — 2"–3" of pea gravel or decorative stone
  • Drainage French drain — 12"+ of clean crushed stone
  • Landscaping beds — 2"–3" of pea gravel or decorative rock
  • Walkways & paths — 3"–4" of compacted gravel

Tons vs. Cubic Yards

  • Most quarries and suppliers price by the ton (weight)
  • Landscaping suppliers sometimes price by the cubic yard (volume)
  • 1 cubic yard of crushed stone ≈ 1.35 tons
  • Always confirm the density with your supplier — it varies by material and moisture content
