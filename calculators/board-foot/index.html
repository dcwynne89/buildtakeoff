How to Calculate Board Feet

A board foot is a unit of lumber measurement equal to 1 inch thick × 12 inches wide × 12 inches long (144 cubic inches). The formula is:

Board Feet = (Thickness × Width × Length) ÷ 144
where thickness and width are in inches, length is in inches (multiply feet × 12)

Examples

  • 2×4×8ft: (2 × 4 × 96) ÷ 144 = 5.33 BF
  • 1×6×12ft: (1 × 6 × 144) ÷ 144 = 6 BF
  • 2×10×16ft: (2 × 10 × 192) ÷ 144 = 26.67 BF

Nominal vs Actual Dimensions

Board feet use nominal dimensions (the stated size), not actual dimensions. A 2×4 is actually 1.5" × 3.5" — but for board foot calculations, you use 2 and 4. This is the industry standard.
