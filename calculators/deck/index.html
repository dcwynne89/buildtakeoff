How to Calculate Deck Boards

To find deck board count: divide the deck width by the board width plus gap, then multiply by the number of boards needed to span the length. Always add 10% for end cuts and waste.

Common Deck Board Spacings

  • 1/4" gap (6mm): Standard for pressure treated lumber — allows for expansion
  • 1/8" gap (3mm): For composite decking or hardwoods like ipe
  • No gap: Butt joint — common with composite hidden fastener systems

Joist Spacing Guidelines

  • 12" OC: For composite decking or spans over 12 ft — maximum rigidity
  • 16" OC: Standard residential — works for most wood and composite products
  • 24" OC: Only for specific composite brands rated for 24" spans
